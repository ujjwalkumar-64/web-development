Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese slices
  • Butter
  • Tomato slices
  • Bread slices

Instructions:

1. Cook the bacon until crispy.

2. Butter one side of each bread slice.

3. Place a slice of cheese, bacon, and tomato between two bread slices (buttered side out).

4. Grill the sandwich until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!
